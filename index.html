About

Systems built around
how your business actually runs

My background is in revenue operations and marketing technology. Not the kind where you pick a tool from a vendor list and configure a few settings — I mean the messy kind, where you're trying to get five different platforms to behave like one coherent system, and you need to understand the business logic before you touch anything.

I start every engagement the same way: by understanding what's actually breaking. What's manual that shouldn't be? Where does data fall out? What's the team doing because there's no other option? Once I have that, the build is straightforward — clean, documented, and built to last beyond the initial handoff.

I've worked with B2B SaaS companies, professional services firms, and e-commerce teams across the US, UK, Australia, and Canada. The tools change. The underlying problems rarely do.
